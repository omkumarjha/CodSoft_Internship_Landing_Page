How much cardio should I be doing?

If your goal is strength and lean muscle gains, you don’t want to do too much cardio.
No daily long jogs if you want to build muscle, because after a certain point, your body will begin to use protein (aka muscle) as fuel.

Should I stretch before my workouts?

Research is mixed on this, so if you don’t want to stretch, you can skip it! Static stretching before a workout has actually been shown to DECREASE speed and performance.
you’re going to stretch before a workout, use this chance to move your body through full range of motion exercises to prepare for the workout you’re about to do.

How To Lose Weight And Tone Up Fast?

You can absolutely burn fat and build muscle at the same time, but it takes conscious nutrition and training.
You’ll ideally want to be in a little bit of a calorie deficit, weight training with challenging weights, and eating sufficient protein.

What should I do about muscle soreness?

Contrary to common belief, sore muscles do not mean that you had a *good workout* and you shouldn’t aim to be seriously sore after each session.I find that when clients are super sore, it hinders their activity level for the rest of the day and days following the workout
